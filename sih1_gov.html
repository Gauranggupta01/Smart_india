Government Schemes for Farmers

Explore the various government schemes available to support farmers in different aspects of farming.

Scheme 1: National Agricultural Development Programme (NADP)

This scheme provides financial assistance to farmers for improving agricultural productivity through various measures such as advanced technology, crop insurance, and infrastructure development.

Learn More

Scheme 2: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

The PMFBY scheme offers crop insurance to farmers to protect them against losses due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Learn More

Scheme 3: Soil Health Management (SHM) Scheme

This scheme focuses on improving soil health through the use of organic fertilizers, soil testing, and other soil management practices.

Learn More

Scheme 4: Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

The KCC scheme provides credit to farmers for their agricultural needs, including buying seeds, fertilizers, and other inputs.
